Bean Counter Bakery Cafe

Bean Counter Bakery Cafe is a renowned bakery and cafe located in Worcester, MA. Specializing in fine cakes, desserts, pastries, and more, the cafe has won multiple awards, including Best of The Knot Hall of Fame and Wedding Wire Bride's Choice. They offer a variety of coffee and espresso drinks brewed from micro-roasted single-origin beans. Open seven days a week, the cafe is a perfect spot for anyone looking to enjoy quality baked goods and coffee.

Category: Bakery & Cafe
