Taqueria Del Pueblo

Taqueria Del Pueblo, situated on Highland Street in Worcester, MA, is a hidden gem for authentic Mexican cuisine. This cozy eatery offers a vibrant atmosphere that instantly transports you to the heart of Mexico. With an extensive menu featuring classics like tacos, burritos, and enchiladas, as well as unique dishes that showcase the rich flavors of Mexican cooking, there's something for everyone to enjoy. The restaurant prides itself on using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create meals that are both delicious and sustainable. Whether you're a WPI student craving a quick bite or someone looking for a family-friendly dining experience, Taqueria Del Pueblo offers a warm welcome and mouth-watering food that will have you coming back for more
