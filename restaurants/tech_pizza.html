Tech Pizza

Tech Pizza is a pizza restaurant located on Highland St in Worcester, MA. The restaurant offers dine-in, takeout, and delivery services. It has received mixed reviews, with a 4.4 rating on Google and a 3.1 rating on Yelp. The place is particularly popular among college students and is described as a "hole in the wall" spot with great food at great prices.
