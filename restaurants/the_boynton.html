The Boynton

The Boynton Restaurant & Spirits is a popular spot in Worcester, MA, known for its extensive menu featuring a variety of American and Italian dishes. They offer a wide range of options, including pizza, pasta, burgers, and seafood. The restaurant also has a full bar, making it a great place for both dining and drinks. The atmosphere is casual and family-friendly, and they offer both indoor and outdoor seating. Perfect for WPI students looking for a diverse menu and a relaxed dining experience.
