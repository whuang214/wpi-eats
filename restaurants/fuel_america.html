Fuel America

Fuel America, located on Grove Street in Worcester, MA, is more than just a coffee shop—it's a community hub where creativity and conversation flow as freely as the coffee. With a modern, industrial-chic interior and a menu that goes beyond your standard coffee fare, Fuel America offers a unique blend of specialty coffees, teas, and a variety of freshly baked goods. Whether you're a WPI student looking for a quiet place to study or a professional in need of a caffeine boost, Fuel America provides a comfortable and inspiring environment. Open seven days a week, it's the perfect spot to fuel your day from morning to evening.
