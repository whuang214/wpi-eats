The Sole Proprietor

The Sole Proprietor, located in Worcester, MA, is a seafood lover's paradise. Known for its upscale ambiance and exceptional service, this restaurant offers a wide range of seafood dishes, from classic New England clam chowder to exotic sushi rolls. The Sole Proprietor is not just a restaurant; it's an experience. With its nautical-themed decor and a bar that serves some of the finest cocktails in town, it's the perfect spot for a romantic dinner or a gathering with friends. Whether you're a WPI student looking to impress a date or simply in the mood for some top-notch seafood, The Sole Proprietor is the place to be.
