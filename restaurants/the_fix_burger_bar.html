The Fix Burger Bar

Nestled in the heart of Worcester, The Fix Burger Bar is a culinary gem that transforms the classic American burger into an extraordinary experience. With locally-sourced beef and over 40 unique toppings, each burger is a customizable masterpiece. Beyond burgers, the restaurant offers an array of craft beers and handcrafted cocktails, all set in a vibrant, modern atmosphere. Weekly events like live music and trivia make it a go-to spot for WPI students and locals alike, offering not just a meal, but a memorable dining adventure.
