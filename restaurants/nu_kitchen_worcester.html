Nu Kitchen Worcester

Nu Kitchen Worcester is a modern cafe that offers a variety of healthy and delicious options. From smoothie bowls and salads to coffee and craft beer, this cafe has something for everyone. The atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming, making it a great spot for both work and social gatherings. Whether you're a WPI student looking for a healthy meal or just someone who enjoys good food and drinks, Nu Kitchen is the place to be.

Category: Cafe
Location: 335 Chandler St, Worcester, MA 01602 Phone: (508) 926-8800
Hours: Monday - Sunday: 8:00 am - 8:00 pm
Price Range: $$ - Moderate
Accepts Goatbucks: No
