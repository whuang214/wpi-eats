Thai Time

Thai Time is a family-owned and operated restaurant in Worcester, MA, that takes pride in offering authentic Thai cuisine. The restaurant has been voted as one of the top 5 local restaurants in Worcester and aims to provide the best Thai food in the area. Their menu features a variety of dishes, including their special Thai Time dish, which is a stir-fry with spicy authentic basil sauce, and the Furious Trio, a combination of pork, chicken, and beef sautéed in spicy Sriracha sauce.
